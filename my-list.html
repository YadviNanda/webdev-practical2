1.
Barbeque Nation

SCO NO 39 ,MADHYA MARG ,SECTOR 26 ,CHANDIGARH

Barbeque Nation has brought to India the unique concept of the live grill. This is the best place for all those people who want to try something different from a regular conventional dishes and are barbecue lovers with that special deviation for smoked food. Barbeque Nation serves a fabulous buffet menu and is perfect for large groups. Their menu has this wide variety of smoky dishes and desserts and is a perfect place to go for all occasions and gatherings. Barbeque Nation is located in Sector 26, East Chandigarh.

Recommendation: Roasted Pinapple

2.
Mainland China

Sco Number 40, Madhya Marg, Chandigarh Sector 26, Chandigarh - 160019, Near Hari Hyper Store

Established in the year 1994, Mainland China in Chandigarh Sector 26, Chandigarh is a top player in the category Chinese Restaurants in the Chandigarh. This well-known establishment acts as a one-stop destination servicing customers both local and from other parts of Chandigarh. Over the course of its journey, this business has established a firm foothold in it’s industry. The belief that customer satisfaction is as important as their products and services, have helped this establishment garner a vast base of customers, which continues to grow by the day. This business employs individuals that are dedicated towards their respective roles and put in a lot of effort to achieve the common vision and larger goals of the company. In the near future, this business aims to expand its line of products and services and cater to a larger client base. In Chandigarh, this establishment occupies a prominent location in Chandigarh Sector 26. It is an effortless task in commuting to this establishment as there are various modes of transport readily available. It is at Madhya Marg, Near Hari Hyper Store, which makes it easy for first-time visitors in locating this establishment. It is known to provide top service in the following categories: Restaurants, Buffet Restaurants, Chinese Restaurants, SeaFood Restaurants, Japanese Restaurants, Fine Dining Restaurants (Rs 1000 To Rs 2000), Pan Asian Restaurants, Thai Restaurants.
